About COSA

Computer Engineering Student's Association

What we do

The Computer Student Association (COSA) is a dynamic organization dedicated to promoting technological education, innovation, and networking opportunities for computer science students. Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, COSA organizes various events, workshops, and competitions throughout the academic year to enhance the technical and soft skills of its members. Our team consists of passionate students who are committed to creating a collaborative environment where members can learn, grow, and excel in their areas of interest.

Our Mission and Vision

An objective to inculcate Teamwork Spirit in students an association of students was needed. The students were informed by the HOD of Computer Department Mr. Sandeep Shukla and Mr. Pramod Patil regarding the formation of a Computer Engineering Students Association (COSA).

Vision

Established an association striving to provide platform for the students to explore and boot their skills, knowledge and the overall development of department and students by organizing various events and activities.

Mission

To organize the Training Program, Seminars, Workshops on latest technology to enhance the Technical Skill-Set of students. To organize the Technical, Non-Technical events to improve the leadership quality among the students. To work in co-operation with all groups and associations who share our aims. To organize Training Programs, Technical and Non-Technical events which will help them for Placements. To motivate and encourage students to have more participation in various events and activities.
